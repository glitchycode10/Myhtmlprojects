Dubnium

Standard State Solid
Atomic Mass 268.126 u
Electron Configuration [Rn]7s25f146d3
Oxidation States 5,4,3
Year discovered 1967


In 1968, a team led by Georgy Flerov at the Russian Joint Institute for Nuclear Research (JINR) bombarded americium with neon and made an isotope of element 105.A highly radioactive metal, of which only a few atoms have ever been made.Dubnium does not occur naturally. It is a transuranium element created by bombarding californium-249 with nitrogen-15 nuclei.
