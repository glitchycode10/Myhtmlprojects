Moscovium

Standard State Solid(expected)
Atomic Mass 290.196 u
Electron Configuration [Rn]7s27p35f146d10
Oxidation States 3,1
Year discovered 2003


IUPAC confirmed the discovery (by scientists from the Joint Institute for Nuclear Research in Dubna, Russia, the Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory in California, USA, and Oak Ridge National Laboratory in Tennessee, USA) in 2015. A highly radioactive metal, of which only a few atoms have ever been made.At present, it is only used in research.
