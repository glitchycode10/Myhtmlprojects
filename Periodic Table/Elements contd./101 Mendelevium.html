Mendelevium

Standard State Solid
Atomic Mass 258.09843 u
Electron Configuration [Rn]7s25f13
Oxidation States +3,+2
Electronegativity (Pauling Scale) 1.3
Ionization Energy 6.58 eV
Melting Point 1100 K
Year discovered 1955


Seventeen atoms of mendelevium were made in 1955 by Albert Ghiorso, Bernard Harvey, Gregory Chopin, Stanley Thompson, and Glenn Seaborg.A radioactive metal, of which only a few atoms have ever been created.Mendelevium does not occur naturally. It is made by bombarding einsteinium with alpha particles (helium ions).
