Sodium

Standard State Solid
Atomic Mass 22.9897693 u
Electron Configuration [Ne]3s1
Oxidation States +1
Electronegativity (Pauling Scale) 0.93
Atomic Radius (van der Waals) 227 pm
Ionization Energy 5.139 eV
Electron Affinity 0.548 eV/td>
Melting Point 370.95 K
Boiling Point 1156 K
Density 0.97 g/cm³
Year discovered 1807


Salt (sodium chloride, NaCl) and soda (sodium carbonate, Na2CO3) had been known since prehistoric times, the former used as a flavouring and preservative, and the latter for glass manufacture.Sodium is a soft metal that tarnishes within seconds of being exposed to the air. It also reacts vigorously with water.Sodium is the sixth most common element on Earth, and makes up 2.6% of the Earth’s crust.Because sodium is so reactive it is never found as the metal in nature. Sodium metal is produced by electrolysis of dry molten sodium chloride.
