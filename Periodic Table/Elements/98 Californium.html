Californium

Standard State Solid
Atomic Mass 251.07959 u
Electron Configuration [Rn]7s25f10
Oxidation States +3
Electronegativity (Pauling Scale) 1.3
Atomic Radius (van der Waals) 245 pm
Ionization Energy 6.30
Melting Point 1173 K
Year discovered 1950


Californium was first made in 1950 at Berkeley, California, by a team consisting of Stanley Thompson, Kenneth Street Jr., Albert Ghiorso, and Glenn Seaborg.Californium is a radioactive metal.Californium did not exist in weighable amounts until ten years after its discovery. It is prepared, in milligram amounts only, by the neutron bombardment of plutonium-239.
