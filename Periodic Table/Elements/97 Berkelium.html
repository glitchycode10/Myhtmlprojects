Berkelium was first produced in December 1949, at the University of California at Berkeley, and was made by Stanley Thompson, Albert Ghiorso, and Glenn Seaborg.Berkelium is a radioactive, silvery metal.Less than a gram of berkelium is made each year. It is made in nuclear reactors by the neutron bombardment of plutonium-239.
