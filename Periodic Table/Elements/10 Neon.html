Neon

Standard State Gas
Atomic Mass 20.180 u
Electron Configuration [He]2s22p6
Oxidation States 0
Atomic Radius (van der Waals) 154 pm
Ionization Energy 21.565 eV
Melting Point 24.56 K
Boiling Point 27.07 K
Density 0.0008999 g/cm³
Year discovered 18986


1898, William Ramsay and Morris Travers at University College London isolated krypton gas by evaporating liquid argon. They had been expecting to find a lighter gas which would fit a niche above argon in the periodic table of the elements and ultimately discovered Neon.It is a colourless, odourless gas. Neon will not react with any other substance.Neon is the fifth most abundant element in the universe. However, it is present in the Earth’s atmosphere at a concentration of just 18 parts per million. It is extracted by fractional distillation of liquid air.
