Germanium was discovered by Clemens A. Winkler at Freiberg, Germany, in 1886. Its existence had been predicted by Mendeleev who predicted its atomic weight would be about 71 and that its density around 5.5 g/cm³. I is a silvery-white semi-metal. It is brittle.Germanium ores are very rare. They are found in small quantities as the minerals germanite and argyrodite.It can also be recovered from the by-products of combustion of certain coals.
